It is a dark time for the whole Rainbow Land. 
Great Rydz sat on the throne in the Pink Mountains 
and turned off the wellspring of the Rainbow River. 
Every citizen is sad. Only hope is in small Teletubbies
who escapes from Citadel of TVP.

You have to collect enough Super Love points to destroy Great Rydz. 
Super Love points can only be collected by killing Enemys, cloned 
sons of Great Rydz. But first you need to find enough Hugs 
to be able to fight with Enemys.
